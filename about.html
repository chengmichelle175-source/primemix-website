About PRIMEMIX

PRIMEMIX is a Sydney-based transport company specializing in concrete transport and site delivery logistics. We also maintain flexible capacity for general freight, allowing our operations to scale as new trucks are added.

Who We Are

We support commercial and industrial projects with safe and punctual transport services. Our focus is straightforward: dependable scheduling, careful handling, and professional standards on every run.

We operate with a compliance-first mindset and stay aligned with Australian heavy vehicle safety practices and requirements.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Deliver reliable concrete and freight transport that keeps projects moving.

Vision: Become the trusted partner for concrete logistics in NSW, expanding into broader freight as our fleet grows.
